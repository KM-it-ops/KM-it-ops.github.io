p.02 — the analyst

Who is this guy?

For eight years — 2015 to 2023 — Michael was an Aviation Security Operations Crew Chief at American Airlines in Charlotte. Federally regulated operations. A security clearance and CBP badge endorsement. International flight operations run under FAA, OSHA, IATA, and CBP requirements, with recurrent incident-response and safety/security training every year.

That job is the foundation: high-accountability documentation, identity validation, and access control — done daily, under real regulators, where sloppy paperwork isn't an option. The habits a SOC wants on day one.

He then converted that discipline into credentials and code: a B.S. in Information Technologies (Cybersecurity concentration) from Southern New Hampshire University — completed December 2025, summa cum laude, 3.96 GPA, inducted into the Sigma Psi chapter of Alpha Sigma Lambda — plus CompTIA Security+ ce (SY0-701), and four public, defense-focused projects.

The through-line: evidence first. Alerts get documented, mappings get cited, uncertainty gets flagged — "No Clear Mapping" is a valid answer, guessing is not.
